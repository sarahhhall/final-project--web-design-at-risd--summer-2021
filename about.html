I'm a recent Brown University grad who loves UI/UX/graphic design and international relations. I'm perpetually inspired by travel (I recently came home from living in Morocco) and the exploration of different cities (I am a sucker for a cool mural and have a weird love of public transportation). I am a huge fan of all things typography and am fascinated by the indecisiveness of the color chartreuse - is it green or yellow? My creative process differs depending on the project, but I always start with the needs and goals of the end user and go from there. I work in Figma, Sketch and the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign), and I am learning HTML and CSS.
